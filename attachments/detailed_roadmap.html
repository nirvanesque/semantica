Risk Mitigation Strategies

Risk 1: Technical complexity of streaming architecture
Mitigation: Prototype early (Month 1), use proven technologies (Kafka), incremental implementation
Risk 2: Contributor onboarding delays
Mitigation: Start onboarding early (Month 3), provide clear documentation, mentorship program
Risk 3: Performance targets not met
Mitigation: Continuous benchmarking, iterative optimization, scalable architecture from start
Risk 4: Multi-modal integration challenges
Mitigation: Use established models (CLIP, BLIP), phased approach, extensive testing
